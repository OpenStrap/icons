# Changelog

## 0.1.0

- Initial release: 19 illustrated icons (17 light+dark pairs, `hrv` and
  `lightSleep` light-only) as 512 px alpha WebP.
- `OsIcon` enum + theme-aware `AppIcon` widget with single-variant and
  missing-asset fallbacks.
- `tool/convert.sh` re-runnable PNG→WebP pipeline with generated asset
  manifest.
